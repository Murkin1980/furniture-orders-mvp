# FAQ

## What does the current platform do?

It receives furniture requests, manages orders and project steps, supports calculators, landing sites, portfolio publishing, and manual AI lead analysis.

## Does AI analyze every request automatically?

No. AI analysis is manual-only and initiated from the admin flow.

## Are prices and commercial terms available?

Not as an approved knowledge source yet. Do not generate or promise them without a source.

## Can production sites be changed freely?

No. Live repositories and deployment bindings must be checked in `LIVE_SITES.md` before changes.
