# Furniture commercial-offer workflow

## Goal

Prepare a clear commercial offer from approved customer and business data.

## Required input

Client/project identity, dimensions, scope, materials, hardware, price source, timing source, payment terms, inclusions, exclusions, and next action.

## Rules

- Never invent a price or legal term.
- Mark missing values as requiring clarification.
- Preserve units, totals, dates, and source references exactly.
- Review the final document before sending it to a client.
