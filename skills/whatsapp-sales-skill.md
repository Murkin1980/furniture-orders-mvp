# WhatsApp sales workflow

## Goal

Move a furniture inquiry toward a useful estimate or measurement without overwhelming the client.

## Collect

- furniture type;
- approximate dimensions;
- room photos or sketch;
- preferred style and materials when known;
- budget range;
- city or area;
- desired timing;
- language;
- next action.

## Rules

- Ask questions in small groups.
- Record unknown values as unknown.
- Never invent prices, deadlines, or guarantees.
- Keep Russian and future Kazakh responses clear and direct.
- Always end with one agreed next action.
