# Design audit workflow

## Goal

Check a public or admin interface before release.

## Audit

1. Is the purpose clear within five seconds?
2. Is the next action clear?
3. Are real trust signals used instead of invented claims?
4. Does the UI follow `DESIGN.md`?
5. Are forms, CTA, validation, and links working?
6. Is the interface usable on mobile?
7. Are text and controls concise?
8. Are loading, empty, error, and fallback states present?
9. Are console errors absent?
10. Is the relevant deployment target verified?

## Result

Fix scoped issues or record them in `SESSION_NOTES.md` with a clear next action.
